MISMATCHED

Mismatched is a 2020 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age romantic drama web series on Netflix, based on Sandhya Menon's 2017 novel When Dimple Met Rishi, adapted by Gazal Dhaliwal and directed by Akarsh Khurana and Nipun Dharmadhikhari. It is produced by Ronnie Screwvala's RSVP Movies.Starring Prajakta Koli, Rohit Saraf, Rannvijay Singha and Vidya Malavade in prominent roles, the series is about Rishi, a die-hard romantic, who believes in traditional ways of dating, falls for Dimple, a gamer, and eventually wanted to marry her. Dhaliwal adapted the series in early 2018, with the script being tweaked for the series. While the novel is set in the United States, the story was adapted to take place in Jaipur, with filming took place in and around the city for 40-50 days. The series features cinematography handled by Avinash Arun and Milind Jog, with editing done by Sanyukta Kaza and Namrata Rao. Mismatched features a soundtrack album, composed by Jasleen Royal, Samar Grewal, Anurag Saikia, Prateek Kuhad, Shashwat Singh, Taaruk Raina, Deepa Unnikrishnan, Abhijay Negi and Hiphop Bhaiya, with Saikia also composing background score for the series. The series premiered on 20 November 2020, through the streaming platform Netflix. It received positive response from audiences, with critics praising the chemistry of Saraf and Koli, the performances of the cast, music and background score, whereas the writing and direction was criticised. Originally the first season consists of six episodes, the series renewed for a second season in November 2020, which is to be conceptualised.[5]
